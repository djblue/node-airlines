Help & FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

   How do I create an account?

Press the "Sign Up" button in the upper right corner of the page. Fill out your personal information and click "Create My Account".

Welcome to the Node Airlines family!

   How do I purchase a ticket?

A Node Airlines account is required to purchase tickets. Once you have logged into your account, enter your current location and desired destination into our search feature. Date and price are optional search terms. Relevant flights will be displayed along with the option to purchase tickets.

   How do I cancel a flight?

A list of your current flights is available on your account userpage. Simply press the "cancel" button to forfeit your seat on a flight. A refund will be credited to the credit card on record**.

** Flights cancelled within 24 hours of the departure time are not eligible for a refund.

   How many bags can I carry on my flight?

You are allowed to carry a maximum of 2 bags per ticket. Each bag is subject to size and weight limitations.

Additional charges apply for overweight and excessively large items.

   What happens if I lose my ticket(s)?

In the event that tickets are lost, guests will only be allowed to board the flight if the owner of the account on which the ticket(s) were purchased is present at the time of departure. Sufficient identification and proof of ticket purchase(s) must be provided.

   What happens if I miss my flight?

Node Airlines encourages the punctuality of its customers. All ticket sales are final once the refund deadline has been reached, 24 hours before the corresponding flight's departure time.

   I left something on the plane. Can I get it back?

Node Airlines is not responsible for lost items. If an item is found that contains sufficient identification, the owner will be notified and the item will be held for a maximum of 30 days.

Items may be shipped via standard post at the owner's expense.
