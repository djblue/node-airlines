    How do I buy a flight?

    * First: log-in.

    * Second: search your flight.

    * Third: click the buy button.

    How do I cancel a flight?

    * First: log-in.

    * Second: check your flights.

    * Third: click the cancel button next to the undesired flight.

    How do I create an account?

    * First: click Sign-up (top right corner).

    * Second: complete the form.

    * Third: click Create my account

    How many bags can I carry on?

    You are allow to carry a maximum of 2 bags per ticket, each bag have some size and weight limitations. Additional charge would apply for overweight and excess items.

    What happen if I lost my ticket/s?

    The person who paid for the ticket/s would have to be present at the time of departure, ID would be required. This only if your ticket is shown on your account.

    What happen if I need to cancel my flight?

    You would get 85% of the ticket price if it is cancel before 72 hrs.

    50% if it is canceled before 48 hrs.

    No refund if it is canceled on the departure day.

    I left something on the plane. Can I get it back?

    We are not responsible for lost items. If we found it and it contains the appropriate ID, we would contact you. otherwise all we have to say that we sorry for your lost.
